Help

This is the help screen with information regarding how to use thef33d.com!


To add a category, click the '+' button at the bottom right of the Menu screen.


To open the Settings Panel for each Category Pane, click the gear image at the bottom right of any Category Pane


To hide a Category Pane, click the eye image at the bottom right of any Category Pane


Add or remove external media accounts to your Feed in your User Panel by going to the Menu, clicking the User icon and selecting which accounts you would like to link.


To select what subscribtions you would like to see in a certain Category Pane, navigate to the Settings Panel, and select which Account to sink in the dropdown menu. Then check which subscriptions you would like to see in the section below and click Save Settings.
